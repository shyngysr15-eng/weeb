Mist Trail

A gentle journey through whispering pines and morning dew.

Duration

2 - 3 Hours

Distance

5.4 km

Elevation

120 m

Experience the serenity

The Mist Trail is designed for those who want to reconnect with nature without the intense physical strain. Located in the lower valley, this path takes you through ancient cedar forests and alongside the Silver Stream.

Perfect for families, photographers, and anyone looking for a peaceful morning escape.
