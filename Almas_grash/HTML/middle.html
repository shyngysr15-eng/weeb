Peak of Solitude

Rise above the clouds and witness the world in silence.

Duration

5 - 6 Hours

Distance

12.8 km

Elevation

850 m

The Panoramic Reward

The Peak of Solitude offers a challenging yet rewarding climb. The trail transitions from dense forest to rocky ridges, ending with a 360-degree view of the Alpine Lake district.

This route requires a decent level of fitness and sturdy hiking boots. It's the perfect choice for those looking to push their limits while enjoying breathtaking scenery.
